about

Some of the finest varieties of cocoa in the world are grown in Colombia. Its climate and lands offer perfect conditions to produce exceptional cocoas. At CACAO we transform cocoa beans from different corners of the country into chocolate bars, dignifying the lives of the farmers dedicated to producing them, and promoting a culture of best planting practices in order to obtain a superior quality raw material.
